# Safa Snigdha | E-Commerce ROI Calculator

Safa Snigdha ROI Calculator is a premium, real-time profit and Return on Investment (ROI) analysis tool designed specifically for e-commerce entrepreneurs and modern online business owners. 

This smart web tool allows users to analyze product profitability instantly without any page refresh, helping businesses make data-driven decisions and control their marketing budgets effectively.

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Real-Time Calculation:** Automatically computes total costs, net profit, and profit margins instantly as you type.
- **Dynamic Arc Gauge:** Visually displays the ROI percentage using an interactive, color-coded modern gauge chart.
- **Cost Ratio Breakdown:** Provides a clean visual bar chart showing the exact ratio of Product Cost, Ad Spend, and Delivery Fees.
- **Local History Log:** Allows business owners to save multiple product calculations to a session history and delete them when needed.
- **One-Click Download:** Export your calculated financial report directly as a high-quality **JPG Image** or **PDF Document** for future reference.

## 🧪 Financial Metrics Tracked

- **Total Cost:** (Product Cost + Ad Spend + Delivery Fee)
- **Net Profit/Loss:** (Selling Price - Total Cost)
- **ROI (Return on Investment):** (Net Profit / Total Cost) * 100
- **Profit Margin:** (Net Profit / Selling Price) * 100
- **Break-Even Point:** Minimum selling price required to avoid a loss.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5** - Structured content and form layouts.

- **JavaScript (ES6+)** - Reactive business logic, mathematical computations, and local history management.
